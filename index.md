# Team UNSURE Presents: "Let's try again tomorrow"

## How to play

### Opening the game
- Find the Version you wish to play.
- Click the link that says Version - Web to play in the web browser.
- Play the game!

### Controls
- Movement: Arrow Keys
- Interact: Space & enter
- Destroy: E
